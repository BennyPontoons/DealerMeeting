Overview

Who the LX Series is for

The LX sits between the S and the M as a style-forward step up. It takes the customizable S platform and elevates it with a sleeker exterior treatment and more standard equipment, for buyers who want their first Bennington to look and feel a class above its price.

If the S is about making the boat yours and the M is about maximum layouts and equipment, the LX is the sweet spot for owners who care most about curb appeal at the dock and a well-equipped helm out of the box.
